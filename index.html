Instructions!

Top Number is about getting the highest number!

Start by rolling the die or set of dice,

You then re-arrange the set of dice from largest number to smallest,
and that becomes your largest number!

You get a total of 3 tries, your third roll will become your largest number,
even if you rolled a bigger number the first or second time.

If you're confident with your first or second roll, you may save that number.

The second player then takes their turn.

The third roll is not shown on screen, but is shown in the score board.

Good Luck and have fun!
